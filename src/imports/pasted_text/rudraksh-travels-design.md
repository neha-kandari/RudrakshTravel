Design a simple, elegant, and premium travel agency website for **Rudraksh Travels** using the provided logo as the main brand inspiration. The website should feel calm, trustworthy, warm, spiritual, and refined, not overly flashy or visually heavy. Use **Satoshi font** throughout the design.

Use a soft luxury color palette inspired by the logo:

* Deep charcoal black: `#080706`
* Rudraksh brown: `#7A3B32`
* Temple gold: `#D8A33A`
* Warm cream: `#F8F1E7`
* Soft sand: `#E8D6BD`
* Muted copper: `#A86145`
* White: `#FFFFFF`

Keep the layout spacious, clean, minimal, and conversion-focused. Avoid oversized visuals, heavy gradients, cluttered sections, and too many decorative elements. Use soft shadows, rounded corners, thin gold borders, clean spacing, and subtle animations.

Create a fully responsive homepage with the following sections:

1. **Header / Navbar**
   Create a simple elegant navbar with the Rudraksh Travels logo on the left, navigation links in the center, and a CTA button on the right.

   Home, Packages, About, Why Choose Us, Contact

CTA button:
**Plan My Trip**

Navbar style:
White or warm cream background, subtle bottom border, soft shadow on scroll, rounded CTA button with Rudraksh brown background and gold border.
